    - `y="760"` empirically centers a 768pt cap-height glyph on a 1024 canvas with most monospaced fonts (cap-height ≈ 70% of font-size; baseline at y=760 places cap-top at ~222, giving ~222px top margin and ~222px bottom margin; rsvg-convert will fall back to system Menlo if JetBrains Mono is not installed system-wide, which is fine since the geometry is what gets rasterized).
