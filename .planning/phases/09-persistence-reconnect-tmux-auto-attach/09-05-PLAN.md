<objective>
Wire every Phase 9 piece together inside `vector-app`: the picker actor constructs `ReconnectableDevTunnelDomain` and hands it to the per-pane spawn; the App listens for `UserEvent::PaneReconnecting` / `PaneReconnected` and maintains `reconnecting_panes`; the per-pane render loop calls `ReconnectPass::update` + `.render` + cell-pipeline glyph compositing for the inline status bar text; the keystroke dispatch gates input on reconnect with the one-shot toast; the tab-title call sites flip to `PaneUiState::Reconnecting` while a pane is reconnecting; pane close cancels the per-pane `CancellationToken` so the reconnect loop exits promptly. The plan closes with a manual smoke UAT checkpoint.

Purpose: This is the integration plan. Plans 09-01..04 produced primitives; this plan binds them into the running app. After this lands, a user closing their laptop lid sees the inline status bar, the tab badge flips, keystrokes are silently dropped (with one toast on the first dropped key), and the pane resumes when the tunnel comes back.
